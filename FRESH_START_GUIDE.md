## 🚀 **Getting Started**

### Step 1: Create Your First Account
1. **Open the application** in your browser
2. **Click "Create Account"** on the login screen
3. **Fill in your details:**
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Account Type (Job Seeker, Recruiter, or Content Creator)
4. **Complete OTP verification**
5. **Set up your profile** (User ID, Password, Role, City)

### Step 2: Start Using CareerGram
- **Create posts** and share your thoughts
- **Upload stories** (24-hour visibility)
- **Connect with other users** when they join
- **Post jobs** (if you're a recruiter)
- **Upload video content** (if you're a content creator)

## 🔑 **Forgot Password Feature**

If you forget your password:
1. **Click "Forgot Password?"** on login screen
2. **Enter your User ID**
3. **Your password will be displayed** (demo mode)
4. **Login form will be auto-filled**

## 🔄 **Reset Application (If Needed)**

If you want to reset everything again:
1. **Create any account and login**
2. **Go to Settings** (⚙️ icon)
3. **Scroll to "Danger Zone"**
4. **Click "🔄 Reset Entire Application"**
5. **Confirm the warnings**
